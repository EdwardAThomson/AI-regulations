## Notes

- The EU AI Act is the centrepiece — phased application runs through August 2027. Prohibited practices applied from Feb 2025; GPAI provisions from Aug 2025; high-risk system provisions from Aug 2026.
- The AI Liability Directive has stalled in trilogue as of early 2026.
- Adjacent legislation (GDPR, DSA, Data Act) is relevant but secondary — prioritise AI-specific instruments first.
- The Council of Europe Framework Convention on AI (Vilnius, 2024) is international, not EU-specific, but the EU and several Member States are signatories.
