# Federal Council Decision on AI Regulation Approach

**Source:** https://www.admin.ch/gov/en/start/documentation/media-releases.msg-id-104110.html
**Date:** 12 February 2025
**Issuing body:** Swiss Federal Council

---

## Decision

On 12 February 2025, the Swiss Federal Council published its approach for regulating artificial intelligence. The Federal Council decided to maintain Switzerland's sector-specific regulatory framework rather than adopting a general cross-sector AI law.

## Key Principles

- Switzerland will **not** adopt a comprehensive AI Act equivalent to the EU AI Act
- The approach is based on ratifying the **Council of Europe AI Convention** and incorporating it into Swiss law
- Legislative changes will focus on areas concerning **fundamental rights**: transparency, data protection, non-discrimination, and supervision
- The private sector will face limited additional obligations
- The framework aims to be innovation-friendly without imposing excessive regulatory burdens

## Regulatory Approach

Instead of creating a standalone AI regulatory regime, Switzerland will:
- Integrate AI considerations into existing sector-specific laws
- Implement the Council of Europe AI Convention through targeted legal amendments
- Develop sector-specific guidance where needed (e.g. healthcare, transport)
- Maintain alignment with key trading partners through an implementation plan

## Timeline and Deliverables

- **FDJP** (Federal Department of Justice and Police) will draft a bill for public consultation by **end of 2026**, implementing the AI Convention
- **DETEC** (together with FDJP, FDFA, and EAER) will develop an **implementation plan by end of 2026** for measures not requiring legislative changes
- The implementation plan will specifically consider Switzerland's alignment with key trading partners

## Strategic Objectives

The Federal Council's regulatory approach aims to:
- Reinforce Switzerland as a centre of innovation
- Safeguard fundamental rights, including economic freedom
- Increase public trust in AI
- Maintain competitiveness relative to key trading partners
